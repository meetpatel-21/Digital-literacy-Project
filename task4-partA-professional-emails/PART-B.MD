# Social Media Checklist for College Students
✅ The Do's
Curate your followers: Only accept requests from people you know or respect.

Highlight achievements: Share internships, projects, and campus involvement.

Use privacy settings: Lock private accounts and turn off location tags.

Think long-term: Assume a future employer or admissions officer will see it.

Verify before sharing: Check sources before reposting news or rumors.


❌ The Don'ts
Don't Post Confidential Information: Avoid sharing internal university documents or private communication.

Don't Engage in Cyberbullying: Maintain a respectful tone and avoid online arguments or offensive language.

Don't Share Private Contact Details: Never post your phone number, home address, or OTPs on public profiles.

Don't Tag Others Without Permission: Respect the privacy of your batchmates and faculty members.

Don't Use Unprofessional Language: Avoid slang or controversial remarks that could negatively impact your reputation.
