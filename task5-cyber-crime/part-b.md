# Project Report Reflection
What surprised me most: Learning how easily cyberbullies exploit fake accounts to destroy reputations—and how UPI fraudsters convincingly impersonate bank officials to steal money. The permanence of digital footprints shocked me most.

One habit I will change: I will stop sharing my live location on Instagram stories and will never click on unsolicited payment links, no matter how urgent they appear.
